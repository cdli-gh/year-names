Babylon Hammu-rabi Year: 30

TranslationYear Hammu-rabi the king, the mighty, the beloved of Marduk, drove away with the supreme power of the great gods the army of Elam who had gathered from the border of Marhaszi, Subartu, Gutium, Tupliasz (Esznunna) and Malgium who had come up in multitudes, and having defeated them in one campaign, he (Hammurabi) secured the foundations of Sumer and Akkad
