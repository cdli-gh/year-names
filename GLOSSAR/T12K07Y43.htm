TranslationYear in which Samsu-iluna the king cut in the great mountains of the land of Amurru ... stones measuring 1 1/2 gar 4 kusz 10 (fingers) (i. e. ca. 11 1/3 m); (he) directed the fullness of the canal (called) 'Samsu-iluna (brings) fullness of abundance' from the heart of a reed swamp to have it flow along a broad course and made the cultivated land of Babylon luxuriant, establishing plenty
