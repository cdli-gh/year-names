Diyala: Esznunna, Tell Asmar Dannum-tahaz
1 mu da-an-na-am-ta-ha-az a-na e2 a-bi-szu i-ru-bu Year in which Dannum-tahaz entered the house of his father (Sumer 34 136, p. 72)
2a mu alan ku3-sig17 ka-ri-bu sza {d}da-nu-ta-ha-az a-na e2-{d}tiszpak i-ru-bu-u2 Year in which Dannum-tahaz brought in the temple of Tiszpak a statue in gold representing himself saying prayers (YOS 14 70)
2ba mu alan igi-du zabar da-nu-um-ta-ha-az a-na e2-{d}tiszpak u2-sze-ri-bu Year in which Dannum-tahaz brought in the temple of Tiszpak a statue in bronze representing himself as leader (OBT IV 65)
2bb mu alan igi-du keszda2 eren2 Year (Dannum-tahaz brought in the temple of Tiszpak) a statue (representing) himself as leader of the drafted troops (Simmons j)
2ca mu alan urudu-zabar ma-hi-syi2 da-nu-ta-ha-az a-na e2-{d}tiszpak Year (Dannum-tahaz brought) in the temple of Tiszpak a statue in bronze representing himself as fighter (YOS 14 22)
2cb mu 1 alan zabar ma-hi-syum Year (Dannum-tahaz brought in the temple of Tiszpak) a statue in bronze (representing himself) as fighter (OBT IV 10b)
2d mu alan szi-ta zabar da-nu-um-ta-ha-az a-na e2-{d}tiszpak u2-sze-ri-bu Year in which (Dannum-tahaz brought) in the temple of Tiszpak a statue in bronze representing him with the szita weapon (Greegus 24 n. 9)
2e mu alan-mesz sza zabar da-an-nu-um-ta-ha-az Year statues in bronze (representing) Dannum-tahaz (Old Bab. Texts Ashmolean, 222)
2f mu zabar da-an-nu-um-ta-ha-az e2-{d}tiszpak u2-sze-lu-u2 Year in which (Dannum-tahaz) erected in the temple of Tiszpak (a statue) in bronze (representing himself as fighter) (Old Bab. Texts Ashmolean, 263)
2g mu 1 alan zabar Year one statue in bronze (OBT IV 10a)
3a mu bad3 nam-dumu-a-ni-sze3 ba-du3 Year the wall (for the house) of his sonship (palace of the hereditary prince) was built (Baqir 6)
3b mu bad3 ma-ru-ti-szu da-an-nu-um-ta-az i-pu-szu Year in which Dannum-tahaz built the wall (of the palace) of his sonship ()
4a mu bara2 mah {d}nerah da-an-nu-um-ta-ha-az Year Dannum-tahaz (made) a magnificent dais for Nerah (Fst Sachs 125)
4b mu {gisz}gu-za bara2 mah {d}nerah Year (Dannum-tahaz made) a magnificent throne dais for Nerah (Simmons 40)
