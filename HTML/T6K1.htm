Ur III Ur-Nammu
1 mu ur-{d}nammu lugal Year Ur-Nammu (became) king
b mu ur-{d}nammu lugal-e sig-ta igi-nim-sze3 gir3 si bi2-sa2-a Year in which Ur-Nammu the king put in order the ways (of the people in the country) from below to above
c mu ur-{d}nammu ni3-si-sa2 kalam-ma mu-ni-gar Year Ur-Nammu made justice in the land
d mu en-{d}inanna unug{ki}-a dumu ur-{d}nammu lugal-a masz-e ba-pad3-da Year in which the son of king Ur-Nammu was chosen by means of the omens as the en-priest of Inanna in Uruk
e mu bad3 ur2i{ki} ba-du3-a Year in which the city wall of Ur was built
f mu lugal-e nibru{ki}-ta nam-lugal szu ba-ti-a Year in which the king received the kingship from Nippur
g mu e2-{d}nanna ba-du3-a Year in which the temple of Nanna was built
h mu en-{d}nanna masz-e ba-pad3-da Year in which the en-priestess of Nanna was chosen by means of the omens
i mu id2-a-{d}nin-tu ba-al Year the canal 'A-Nintu / water of Nintu' was dug
j mu nin-dingir-{d}iszkur masz-e pad3-da Year in which the high-priestess of Iszkur was chosen by means of the omens
k mu gu-ti-um{ki} ba-hul Year Gutium was destroyed
l mu e2-{d}nin-sun2 ur2i{ki}-a ba-du3-a Year in which the temple of Ninsun in Ur was built
m mu e2-{d}en-lil2-la2 ba-du3-a Year in which the temple of Enlil was built
n mu id2-en-erin2-nun ba-ba-al-la Year in which the canal 'en-erin-nun' was dug (Cros, NFT AO 4308)
o mu {gisz}gigir {d}nin-lil2 ba-dim2-ma Year in which the chariot of Ninlil was made
p mu {d}lugal-ba-gara2 e2-a-na ku4-ra Year in which the god Lugal-bagara was brought into his temple (BM 17822, 20776)
q mu {d}lugal-ba-gara2 e2-a ku4-ra us2-sa Year after the year in which the god Lugal-bagara was brought into the temple (U 8846)
