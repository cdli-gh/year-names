3 mu ba-szi-mi{ki} ba-hul Year Baszimi was destroyed

8 mu alan urudu gu-la e2-{d}utu-sze3 i-ni-in-ku4-re Year (Gungunum) brought into the temple of Utu a great statue in copper
9 mu en-{d}utu ba-hun-ga2 Year in which the en-priestess of Utu was installed
10 mu {gisz}szu-nir gal nesag-ga2 2-a-bi u3 {gisz}gu-za kaskal e2-{d}nanna-sze3 i-ni-in-ku4-re Year (Gungunum) brought into the temple of Nanna two great emblems as a present / offering and a sedan-chair

28 mu us2-sa id2-{d}ba-ba6-he2-gal2 id2 gir2-su{ki} ba-ba-al Year after the year the canal of Girsu (called) 'Baba is abundance' was dug
